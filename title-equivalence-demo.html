Four of these five candidates do the same real job as the one whose title happened to match. A keyword filter never sees them. It isn't that the filter is careless — a static keyword list structurally cannot keep pace with how many ways one job gets named across companies and industries. Reading for behavior, not title, is what closes that gap.
